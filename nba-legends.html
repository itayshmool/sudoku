Who's Who
The 9 Legends of Hardwood Sudoku

Nine icons of basketball history — from the greatest of all time to the next generation of superstars. Tap any card to see their story.

Icon #1 · Jersey #23
Michael Jordan
Chicago Bulls
  • 6× NBA Champion — two three-peats with the Bulls (1991–93, 1996–98)
  • 6× Finals MVP — never lost a Finals series in his career
  • 5× League MVP & 10× scoring champion
  • Retired with a 30.1 ppg average — highest in NBA history
  • The original Air Jordan — changed sneaker culture forever
Signature: Tongue-out dunk
Icon #2 · Jersey #32
Magic Johnson
Los Angeles Lakers
  • 5× NBA Champion with the "Showtime" Lakers
  • 3× Finals MVP & 3× League MVP
  • Led the 1992 Dream Team at the Barcelona Olympics
  • Revolutionized the point guard position at 6'9"
  • Famous for his no-look pass — pure basketball magic
Signature: The no-look pass
Icon #3 · Jersey #33
Larry Bird
Boston Celtics
  • 3× NBA Champion with the Celtics (1981, 1984, 1986)
  • 3× League MVP (1984–86) — dominant stretch
  • Legendary trash-talker who always backed it up
  • Two-time All-Star Game MVP including the first 3-point contest
  • One of basketball's greatest pure shooters ever
Signature: Cold-blooded jumper
Icon #4 · Jersey #23
LeBron James
Cleveland Cavaliers
  • 4× NBA Champion with 3 different franchises
  • 4× Finals MVP & 4× League MVP
  • The NBA's all-time scoring leader — surpassed Kareem in 2023
  • Won titles with Miami, Cleveland & LA — true winner everywhere
  • First player to score 40,000 career points
Signature: The headband
Icon #5 · Jersey #24
Kobe Bryant
Los Angeles Lakers
  • 5× NBA Champion with the Lakers
  • Scored 81 points in a single game — 2nd all-time ever
  • 2× Finals MVP & 1× League MVP (2008)
  • 18× All-Star — won 4 All-Star Game MVP awards
  • The "Black Mamba" mentality — unmatched competitive drive
Signature: The fadeaway
Icon #6 · Jersey #34
Shaquille O'Neal
Los Angeles Lakers
  • 4× NBA Champion — three-peat with Lakers (2000–02)
  • 3× Finals MVP & 1× League MVP (2000)
  • At 7'1" and 325 lbs — one of the most physically dominant players ever
  • 15× All-Star, 2× scoring champion
  • Nobody could stop the Shaq Attack — just had to foul him
Signature: The Shaq Dunk™
Icon #7 · Jersey #33
Kareem Abdul-Jabbar
Los Angeles Lakers
  • 6× NBA Champion — won at Milwaukee & LA
  • Record 6× League MVP — more than anyone in history
  • 19× All-Star over a staggering 20-year career
  • Held the all-time scoring record for 38 years (until LeBron, 2023)
  • Inventor of the skyhook — the most unstoppable shot in basketball
Signature: The skyhook
Icon #8 · Jersey #30
Stephen Curry
Golden State Warriors
  • 4× NBA Champion with the Golden State Warriors
  • 2× MVP including the first-ever unanimous MVP (2016)
  • Holds the record for most 3-pointers in a season (402) and career
  • Revolutionized basketball — made the logo 3 a regular shot
  • The mouth guard is always hanging — his signature nervous habit
Signature: Deep-range three
Icon #9 · Jersey #8
Deni Avdija
Portland Trail Blazers
  • Israeli basketball star — #9 pick in the 2020 NBA Draft
  • Played for Maccabi Tel Aviv before entering the NBA
  • Traded to the Portland Trail Blazers in 2024, thriving as a starter
  • Stars for the Israeli national team — a source of national pride
  • Unmistakable for his massive curly afro — the most recognizable hair in the NBA
Signature: The afro
